Interests. Stuff I like

Science

Is not only an occupation but also a hobby. I'm interested in all kind of technological and science related topics. I think that comprehensive scientific knowledge really helps us in our daily life.

Music

I'm one of those persons with a life soundtrack, always listening to music. I feel excited whenever I come across new songs, whatever the musical genre. I have also taken some first steps towards learning to play the guitar.

Cooking

In my family we have always enjoyed good food and both of my grandmas were great cooks. Thus I became fond of cooking. I also started our own family recipes blog in 2008.

Web programming

I used to think that web development was completely different to writing code for numerical calculations, but at the end of the day the languages are more alike than different. I have been able to transfer my analytical programming intuition to web design.

Sports

I try to keep an active daily live. Swimming, scuba diving, snowboarding, body combat and yoga are among my favourite sports.

Travel

Any unvisited place is a good destination for a trip: nearby villages or remote natural landscapes. I just enjoy seeing new places.

Photography

I have always been attracted to the artistic power of photography. I really enjoy a good exhibition regardless its topic. I also like to hang around with my camera, even though I'm just an amateur.

Reading

An alternative way to travel, to get to know other lifestyles, to discover fantasy landscapes, to strengthen your thoughts or change your mind about them. Reading makes you free.
